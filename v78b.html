Key Assumptions & Notes

  • All data presented, including sector performance, market weight, and technical indicators, is based on the most recent available market close information. Refer to the timestamp at the top of the page for specifics.
  • Daily performance ('change_pct') reflects the percentage change from the previous trading day's close to the current day's close.
  • The historical heatmap displays weekly performance data. Weeks with no data are represented by a neutral gray color.
  • Technical indicators like RSI, MFI, and OBV are provided for informational purposes. They are standard tools for market analysis but are not predictive and do not guarantee future results.
  • Market weight represents a sector's proportion of the total market capitalization of the S&P 500 index.
  • The commentary and insights are AI generated based on the provided data and are intended to highlight significant market movements, not to serve as specific trading recommendations.
